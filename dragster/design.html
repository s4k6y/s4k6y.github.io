Step 2: Design

Once more, we used SketchUp for Education for our designs. The designing in SketchUp was not super complex, pretty much being one piece of wood with some holes (axles and CO2 canister). However, the curves and angles of the shape meant I had to use a new technique called 'intersecting faces' to get them correct. I followed the specifications for dimensions provided by Mr. Doherty and tried to make an aerodynamic, visually pleasing design. The small holes through the middle are for the axles and the large one in the back is for a CO2 can or bottle that would propel the dragster forward.
